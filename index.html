Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is Fluid a full CRM?

Fluid is a visual sales pipeline. It gives you the structure you need to track deals and follow-ups without the complexity of a traditional CRM. If you need advanced reporting, marketing automation, or enterprise features, Fluid probably isn't the right fit. If you need clarity and simplicity, it is.

Can I import my data from a spreadsheet?

Yes. You can import contacts and deals from CSV files. The import takes a few minutes and maps your columns automatically.

How is Fluid different from HubSpot or Pipedrive?

Those tools are built for large teams with dedicated sales ops. They come with a learning curve, setup time, and features most small teams never use. Fluid is intentionally simple - it does less, but what it does, it does well.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. You get full access to everything for 14 days, no credit card required. If it helps you stay organized, upgrade. If not, no hard feelings.

Can I use Fluid with my team?

Yes. Invite teammates, assign deals, and see everyone's pipeline in one place. No per-seat pricing traps.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You can export all your data anytime. Nothing gets locked in.
